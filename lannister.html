AThe Gold Tooth

The Golden Tooth is a stronghold in the Westerlands. It is the seat of House Lefford, a noble house sworn to House Lannister of Casterly Rock. It is located near the River Road, in the pass between two mountainous areas, northeast of Casterly Rock and southwest of Riverrun, close to the borders between the Westerlands and the Riverlands.
